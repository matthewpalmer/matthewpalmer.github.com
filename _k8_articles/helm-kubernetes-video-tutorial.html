Helm and Kubernetes Introduction – Video Tutorial

I recorded a video tutorial to introduce Node.js, Ruby, and PHP developers to Helm for Kubernetes.

In this guide, we cover an overview of Helm’s concepts – Charts and Releases. We also go through the Helm architecture. Finally, we walk through a code example of converting a regular Node.js and MongoDB web application into a Helm Chart.
