Charter

Read the Swift mailing lists on your iPhone or iPad.

Charter is an iOS app that makes reading the Swift mailing lists easy.

  • Read the Swift Evolution, Swift Users, and Swift Dev mailing lists
  • Message replies are threaded together to make discussion easy to follow
  • Download the list contents to read online and offline
  • Excessively long quotes in emails are truncated to remove noise

(Side note: I shipped the 1.0 for Charter way earlier than I normally would, so the first version isn’t that great. The good news is that work’s well underway for the next version. If you have any features you’d love to see included, let me know on Twitter. Also, I’m thinking of open sourcing the app, so if that’s something you want to see, let me know as well.)
