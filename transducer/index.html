v1.0 — OS X 10.11+ (El Capitan)

You have to go through some dumb 💩 to post a podcast. Transducer’s a Mac app to make that a bit better.

Your current workflow probably has more crazy steps than an Escher painting. We take a couple out.

  1. Choose your podcast
  2. Set the episode’s title and description
  3. Choose your host
  4. Click upload

Ever had a nerd email you about missing MP3 ID3 tags? (Don’t know what those acronyms are?) Get those metadata weirdos off your back—Transducer handles it all for you.

A bunch of other small points to convince you to try out the app:

  • Run custom scripts after your show’s uploaded, like creating a new Wordpress or Jekyll post for the episode
  • Never forget the number of the episode you’re up to, like I always do
  • All the info you need for your RSS feed is right there, ready to be copy-and-pasted
  • Never log into your file host’s upload page again
  • No migration. Your podcast stays where it is, your feed stays as it is. Transducer works with any SoundCloud or Libsyn account.
  • Works with MP3 files
